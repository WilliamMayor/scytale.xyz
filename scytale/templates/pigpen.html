Pig Pen Cipher

The Pig Pen cipher is a fun cipher that encrpyts letter in a very similar way to the mixed alphabet cipher. The difference is that letters are substituted for symbols instead of letters. This makes encrypted messages look very odd and complicated.

The instruction sheet contains a good example of how pig pen works. Draw grids of shapes and fill the shapes with letters. The lines that enclose a letter form its symbol. If a symbol is duplicated then add a dot to it so you can tell the difference between the encrypted symbols.

Rail Fence Cipher

Rail Fence ciphers are interesting transposition ciphers that involve writing a message in a zig zag across multiple "rails".

The key for a Rail Fence cipher is simply the number of "rails" you wish to use.

To encrypt a message write it, letter by letter, in a zig zag shape. The numbers of rails you pick determines the depth of the zig zag. When you have written your message out simply read off the rows, one at a time from top to bottom.

To decrypt a message write it onto a zig zag shape created using the same number of rails. Then read off the zig zag.
